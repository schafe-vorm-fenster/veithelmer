## Download Instructions

### For poster.jpg:
1. Visit ČSFD.cz or jip film & verleih sources
2. Find official cinema poster (portrait orientation)
3. Prefer the standard theatrical poster over special editions
4. Right-click → Save Image, or contact production company
5. Minimum resolution: 600px width
6. Place in: `content/films/gondola/poster.jpg`

### For teaser.jpg:
1. Look for cable car imagery (key visual from film)
2. Wide-format still showing Georgian mountain landscape
3. Should feature the gondolas or the main characters
4. 16:9 ratio landscape preferred
5. Minimum resolution: 1280x720px
6. Place in: `content/films/gondola/teaser.jpg`

## Image Specifications
- Format: JPEG (.jpg)
- Poster: Portrait, minimum 600x900px, 2:3 ratio preferred
- Teaser: Landscape, minimum 1280x720px, 16:9 ratio preferred
- Quality: 80-90% JPEG compression
- Color: RGB color space

## Key Visual Elements to Look For
- Cable car/gondola imagery
- Georgian mountain landscapes
- Two main characters (Iva and Nino)
- Romantic, poetic atmosphere
- Pastel colors (especially in Korean poster variant)
- Wordless/silent film aesthetic
