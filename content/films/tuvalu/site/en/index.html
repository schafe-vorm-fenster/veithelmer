Tuvalu - A Film by Veit Helmer

Tuvalu — is the name of the island the characters dream of as their refuge. A dream whose fulfillment seems unlikely, considering that they are cut off from the outside world in a run-down and largely deserted swimming pool.

Denis Lavant (Les Amants du Pont Neuf) plays the oppressed younger son of the pool owner, and he does his utmost to maintain the illusion that this amazing pile of ruins is still a thriving business. In reality, there are hardly any swimmers, and those who come literally pay in buttons.

Meanwhile, his unscrupulous older brother is trying to bring about the final ruin of the business so that the building can be torn down and the land redeveloped.
