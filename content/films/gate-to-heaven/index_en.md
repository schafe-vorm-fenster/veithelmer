Veit Helmer's second feature film "Gate to Heaven" (starring Miki Manojlović and Udo Kier) was filmed in 2002 at Frankfurt Airport.

The film tells the story of people living in the gray zone of the transit area of a large international airport - a modern fairy tale about dreams, hope, and life between worlds.
