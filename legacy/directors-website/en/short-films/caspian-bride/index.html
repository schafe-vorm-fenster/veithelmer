Caspian Bride

Caspian Bride

A lonesome man takes a ride to a dump in search for usable things.

Hidden under a lot of rubbish he finds an old mannequin. He takes her along to his hut, washes and clothes her. He shaves and goes on a bike tour through the oil fields. When a group of children runs after him, he collides with a flock of sheep. The mannequin sinks in a puddle of oil. This story could have a sad ending ... But at that moment he encounters his true love.

Country, year: Azerbaijan / Germany 2005
Format: 8 min, 35 mm, Colour, Dolby Digital
Cast: Günesh Alieva, Gurban Mesimov
Director: Veit Helmer
Cinematographer: Elman Aliev
Editing: Moritz Steinkohl
Music: Rosid Behbudov
Colouring: Ron Geipel
