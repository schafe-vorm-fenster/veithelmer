Jürgen Knieper COMPOSER  
     
Since the 1970s Jürgen Knieper has been counted among the most sought after film music composers in Germany. He has gained recognition, in particular, for his work on many of Wim Wender’s films. He became known to a wider public following his work on the ARD satire series “Scheibenwischer” (“Windscreen Wiper”).
