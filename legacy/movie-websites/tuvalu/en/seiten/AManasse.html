Alexander Manasse PRODUCTION DESIGN  
     
Alexander Manasse (born 1962) completed a Masters in philosophy, arts and art history 1982-1986. Together with numerous television programs and commercials he has also worked on music videos and drawn storyboards. WORKS (Selection):

In 1984 Alexander Manasse designed the set for Tom Tykwer’s “Tödliche Maria” (“Deadly Maria”).

He won Best Production Design at the 1997 Festival de Gijon for “Winterschläfer” (“Wintersleepers”). Collaboration with Tom Tykwer continued in 1998 with the hit film “Lola rennt” (“Run, Lola Run”).

Following that he worked with Werner Masten on “Der Tanker” (The Tanker, 1999), and at the moment is working with Iwan Fila on “König der Diebe” (“King of Thieves”) and with Pepe Planitzer on “Ein Schiff wird kommen” (“A Ship Is Coming”).

Alexander Manasse was awarded the prize for Best Production Design for “TUVALU” at the 1999 Internationalen Hofer Filmtagen (Hof International Film Festival).
