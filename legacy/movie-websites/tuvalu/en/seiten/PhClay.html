Philippe Clay KARL  
         
 
Paris-born Philippe Clay can look back on a long and distinguished career, and not just as an actor. In his homeland he is regarded as a chanson legend.

Since 1945 Clay has performed on all the main stages of French theatre. The last oc-casion was in 1999 at the Festival of Anjou in the production of “Le sexe faible” (“The Weaker Sex”) with his acting colleague Jean-Claude Brialy.

Since 1966 he has been a regular face on French television screens; to the present day he has played in more than 50 TV films.

In 1954 Clay made his screen debut in Jean Renoir’s FRENCH CAN-CAN. German cinema-goers last saw him in 1999 in the artist biography TOULOUSE-LAUTREC.
