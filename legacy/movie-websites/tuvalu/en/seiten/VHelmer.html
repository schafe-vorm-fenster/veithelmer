Veit Helmer
DIRECTOR & PRODUCER
   
Veit Helmer has been making films, since he was 14. He studied directing at the Munich Film School, co-produced "Die Brüder Skladanowsky" by Wim Wenders and is best known for his offbeat short films, which earned him more than 50 awards at all major international film festivals. Beside his film work, he has directed various commercials, for brands such as Coca-Cola USA, IKEA France and Brooklyn Italy (Agency: BBH). "Tuvalu" is his first feature film. His second feature film “Gate to Heaven", starring Miki Manojlovic and Udo Kier was released in December 2003 in Germany by Prokino/20th Century Fox. His first documentary “Casting in Hollywood is currently in post-production. Veit Helmer is 37 years old and lives in Berlin.
