Gate to Heaven

What would life be without dreams?

Story

In the no-man's-land of a large international airport's transit area, destinies from all over the world meet.

Here, where time seems to stand still and usual rules don't apply, unusual friendships and absurd situations emerge.

The protagonists – refugees, businessmen, dreamers – all are trapped in this in-between space, between departure and arrival, between yesterday and tomorrow.

With much humor and poetry, "Gate to Heaven" tells of humanity's ability to maintain hope even under the most adverse circumstances.
